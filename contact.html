Quick Answers

Frequently Asked

How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery takes 4–7 business days across India. Express delivery (2–3 days) is available at checkout for an additional fee. Metro cities like Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore typically receive orders faster.
What is your return policy?
We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for unopened, unused items in original packaging. Simply email us at orders@lumiere.in with your order ID and reason, and we'll arrange a free pickup.
Do you offer gift wrapping?
Yes! Add our signature gift wrapping at checkout for ₹199. We use recycled kraft paper with a hand-tied satin ribbon, and can include a personalised message card at no extra charge.
Do you ship internationally?
Currently we ship within India only. We're working on expanding to UAE, Singapore, and the UK in late 2026. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when international shipping launches.
Are your products eco-friendly?
Sustainability is at the core of everything we do. We source from certified artisans, use biodegradable packaging, and offset our carbon footprint through verified reforestation projects in India.
Can I track my order?
Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking link. You can also track your parcel directly on our website or WhatsApp us your order ID for a live update from our team.
